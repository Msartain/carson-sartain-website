Who I Am

I’m curious, I’m thoughtful and, for as long as I can remember, I’ve been a storyteller. I’m obsessively interested in understanding the world around me, but not just how I see it. I want to know what makes us tick, how the same events and experiences land across the spectrum, and what—despite the multitude of differences between us—really binds us together. Time and time again, I’ve found that common ground in storytelling. It’s where we’re able to build compassion, where we become inspired to take action, and where we set the gears in motion for creating positive impact and experiences. And I’m someone who’s into making all of that happen.

What I Do

Every element of my career has been rooted in the cultivation of genuine connection—whether to the ceaseless work of environmental nonprofits, to the critical conversations global media organizations and their audiences are bringing to the fore, or to technology companies leveraging their platforms to address the most pressing issues of our time. Connection like this paves the way for empathy, ideas, and progress—and by developing and delivering marketing and communications strategies that are guided by creativity, strategy, and the human experience, my work supports these outcomes.
